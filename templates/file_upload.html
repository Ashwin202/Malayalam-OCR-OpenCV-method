About Project

A free online OCR (Optical Character Recognition) service, that can analyze text in the file that you upload, and then detect and convert the text to an editable format.

How to use OCR on PDF and image:
Step 1.
Select your file in our free online OCR scanner.
Step 2.
Click the Characterise button to start the OCR conversion.
Step 3.
Get the detected file.
